# Comparison Report - Event2

## Shared Facts

- The US is withdrawing 5,000 troops from Germany
- The withdrawal is expected to be completed over the next 6 to 12 months
- The US has around 36,000 troops stationed in Germany
- The decision has been met with concern from US Republican lawmakers and European allies
- Germany's Defence Minister Boris Pistorius has stated that the presence of American soldiers in Europe is in the interest of both Germany and the US

## Contradictions

- The reason behind the US troop withdrawal from Germany is unclear, with different explanations provided by the Pentagon and President Trump
- The impact of the troop withdrawal on US commitment to European security is disputed, with some officials saying it will undermine deterrence and others saying it will have limited impact

## Summary

The US has announced the withdrawal of 5,000 troops from Germany, a decision met with concern from US Republican lawmakers and European allies, who fear it may undermine deterrence and send the wrong signal to Russia, while others argue it is an opportunity for Europe to take on more responsibility for its own security.
