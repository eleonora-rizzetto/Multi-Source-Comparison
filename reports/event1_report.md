- Musk is suing OpenAI, Altman, and other defendants, including Microsoft, over claims that they strayed from the company's original mission and misled him.
- The trial is taking place in a federal courtroom in California, with Judge Yvonne Gonzalez Rogers presiding.
- Musk and Altman will testify in the trial, which may determine the future of AI development.
